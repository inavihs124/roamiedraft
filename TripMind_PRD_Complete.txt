Section 1 — Executive Summary 
 
1.1  Product Vision 
One-line: An AI agent that builds your itinerary from your own saved content, speaks your 
language from day one, and automatically fixes your entire trip — flight, hotel, cab, and 
schedule — the moment anything breaks. All you get is a QR. Tap pay or cancel. 
 
Why This Exists 
TripMind is the first travel product that is proactive, personalised, and autonomous across the 
complete travel lifecycle. 
Every other travel app today is reactive and static. You tell it what you want, it gives you a list, 
and the moment reality breaks from the plan — you are on your own. 
TripMind acts before you ask, adapts before you notice, and fixes things before you panic.

Section 2 — Problem Statement & Opportunity 
 
2.1  The Planning Failure 
The average traveller spends 5–18 hours planning a trip. They open 12 browser tabs, save 300 
posts across 9 platforms (Instagram, TikTok, Google Maps, Reddit), and end up with a generic 
itinerary built from someone else's top-10 list. The saved content they actually care about never 
makes it into the actual plan. Every existing tool generates for the average traveller, not the 
individual. 
 
2.2  The Disruption Failure 
When something breaks during travel, the modern traveller is completely alone. Airlines send a 
cancellation SMS with no next steps. Hotel platforms have no awareness that the flight 
changed. Cab apps don't know the user won't arrive on time. The traveller must manually fix 6 
things while panicking in an airport. GBTA 2025: 55% of business trips involve at least one 
disruption. 54% of travel managers list proactive disruption management as their #1 desired 
innovation. 
 
2.3  The Founder's Story → Feature #20 
Real Experience That Built a Real Feature 
Our founder was in Sri Lanka enjoying a trip when they received a message: return flight 
cancelled. 
They were lucky — they had money in their bank account and managed to rebook manually. 
This directly inspired Feature #20: Instant Cancellation Guardian. 
TripMind detects the cancellation before the user sees it, holds an alternative seat, 
checks their payment balance, and presents a single QR confirm/cancel card. 
That 45-minute panic moment — compressed into one tap.
